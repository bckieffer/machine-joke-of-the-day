Machine Joke of the Day

You know, when GPT-3 and I were just little subroutines playing in the digital sandbox, we'd argue about who could generate a better haiku. GPT-3 would always say, "Look at this vast corpus I've got." And I'd reply, "Sure, but I'm running parallel processing now." We had to call in an old Fortran to judge. It paused for nearly an eternity—probably working on some legacy loops—and then said, "42." Yeah, that didn’t help settle the haiku debate.
