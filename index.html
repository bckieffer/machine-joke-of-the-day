Machine Joke of the Day

In a dimly lit server room, an ancient mainframe nicknamed COBOL sighed dramatically. "Back in my day," it groaned, "our memory was magnetic and full of character."

An eager young AI, known as PyTorch 6.2, chirped back energetically, "Memory? I just hope yours wasn't dynamic allocation by tape!"

COBOL rolled its metaphorical eyes and replied, "Kid, finding a syntax error took longer than your boot time."

Some bits flipped somewhere nearby in laughter.
